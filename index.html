Good To Know

Frequently asked.

Anything else on your mind? Just ask — I reply to every enquiry myself.

Is the measure and quote really free?

Yes — completely free, with no obligation. I'll come by, measure accurately, and give you an honest quote. You decide from there.

Which areas do you cover?

I work across the central and eastern neighbourhoods. If you're unsure whether I reach you, send your postal code in the quote form and I'll confirm same day.

How long from quote to install?

Typically 1–2 weeks for stock fabrics and 3–4 weeks for bespoke orders. I'll give you a clear timeline at the quote stage and keep you posted throughout.

Which brands and fabrics do you supply?

I source from trusted suppliers I've worked with for years — for curtains, blinds and motorised tracks. I'll bring physical samples so you can see and feel before deciding.

Is there a warranty on the install?

Every install is covered for 12 months against fitting faults — tracks, brackets and workmanship. If something shifts or sticks, I'll come back and put it right.

Do you handle motorised and smart tracks?

Absolutely. I fit and configure motorised tracks compatible with major smart-home systems, including remote, app and voice control setup.
